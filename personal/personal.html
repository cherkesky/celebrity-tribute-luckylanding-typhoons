Some info about our beloved queen

The House of Windsor as we know it today began in 1917 when the family changed its name from the German “Saxe-Coburg-Gotha.” Queen Elizabeth's grandfather, King George V, was the first Windsor monarch, and today's working royals are the descendants of King George and his wife, Queen Mary.

  • Birthday: 21 April 1926
  • Full Name: Elizabeth Alexandra Mary Windsor
  • Country of current residence: Buckingham Palace, London, United Kingdom
  • immediate family members:
    • Father: George VI
    • Mother: Elizabeth Bowes-Lyon
    • Sibling: Princess Margaret
    • Spouse: Prince Philip, Duke of Edinburgh (m. 1947)
    • Issue: Charles, Prince of Wales
    • Issue: Anne, Princess Royal
    • Issue: Prince Andrew, Duke of York
    • Issue: Prince Edward, Earl of Wessex
